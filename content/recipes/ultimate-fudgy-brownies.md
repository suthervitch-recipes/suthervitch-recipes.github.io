title: Ultimate Fudgy Brownies
description: 'These homemade brownies are rich, fudgy, and have that perfect crinkly top. They are incredibly easy to make from scratch!'
portions: 16 squares
prepTime: 15 mins
cookTime: 25 mins

ingredients:
  - 140g (10T) unsalted butter, melted
  - 200g (1c) granulated sugar
  - 2 large eggs
  - 5ml (1t) vanilla extract
  - 65g (½c) all-purpose flour
  - 50g (½c) Dutch-process cocoa powder
  - 2g (¼t) sea salt
  - 90g (½c) chocolate chips
notes: |-
  From https://www.loveandlemons.com/brownies-recipe/

  #### **Author Notes:**
  - Be careful not to overbake your brownies if you want them to stay perfectly fudgy in the center. 
  - Using high-quality cocoa powder makes a significant difference in the deep chocolate flavor profile of this recipe.

1 Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F). Line a 20cm x 20cm (8" x 8") baking pan with parchment paper.

2 In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter and granulated sugar until well combined. 

3 Add the eggs one at a time, whisking vigorously after each addition, then stir in the vanilla extract.

4 Sift the all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, and sea salt into the wet ingredients. Fold gently with a spatula just until combined; do not overmix.

5 Fold in the chocolate chips. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top.

6 Bake for 22 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs. Let cool completely before slicing.
