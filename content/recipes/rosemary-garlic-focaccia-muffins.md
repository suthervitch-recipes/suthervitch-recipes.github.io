---
title: Rosemary Garlic Focaccia Muffins
description: 'These Rosemary Garlic Focaccia Muffins are crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside, and packed with savory herbal flavor.'
portions: 12 muffins
prepTime: 20 mins
cookTime: 20-25 mins
cover: null
ingredients:
  - 420g (3½c) all-purpose flour
  - 7g (2¼t) active dry yeast
  - 10g (2t) sugar
  - 5g (1t) salt
  - 355ml (1½c) warm water
  - 60ml (¼c) extra virgin olive oil, divided
  - 30g (2T) fresh rosemary, chopped
  - 4 cloves garlic, minced
  - Flaky sea salt, for topping
notes: |-
  From https://saltnsprinkles.com/rosemary-garlic-focaccia-muffins/

  #### **Author Notes:**
  - Make sure your water is warm to the touch but not hot, otherwise it can kill the yeast.
  - You can customize these with chopped olives, cherry tomatoes, or a sprinkle of vegan parmesan if desired.

1 In a large bowl, whisk together the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 to 10 minutes until it becomes frothy.

2 Stir in 30ml (2T) of olive oil, the flour, and the salt until a sticky dough forms. Cover the bowl and let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.

3 Punch down the dough. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin generously with half of the remaining olive oil. Divide the dough evenly into the 12 muffin cups.

4 Press your fingers into each muffin to create deep dimples. Drizzle the remaining olive oil over the tops, then scatter the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, and flaky sea salt evenly across the muffins.

5 Let the muffins rest for another 15 to 20 minutes while you preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).

6 Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until the tops are golden brown and crisp. Allow them to cool slightly in the pan before removing.
