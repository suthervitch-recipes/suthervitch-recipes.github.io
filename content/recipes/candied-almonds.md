---
title: Candied Almonds
description: 'Crunchy, sweet, cinnamon-spiced roasted almonds.'
portions: 8 servings
prepTime: 5 mins
cookTime: 1 hour
cover: null
ingredients:
  - 1 egg white
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 3 cups raw whole almonds
  - 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  - 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  - 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
notes: |-
  From https://joyfoodsunshine.com/candied-almonds/
  
  Stir the almonds every 15 minutes while baking to ensure they roast evenly and don't clump together.
tags:
  - Dessert
  - Vegetarian
---

1 Preheat your oven to 250°F (120°C). Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

2 In a large bowl, whisk the egg white and vanilla extract together until very frothy.

3 Add the raw almonds to the egg white mixture and toss until every almond is well coated.

4 In a separate small bowl, mix together the granulated sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt.

5 Pour the dry sugar mixture over the coated almonds and toss until the almonds are evenly covered in the cinnamon sugar.

6 Spread the coated almonds in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet.

7 Bake for 1 hour, stirring thoroughly every 15 minutes.

8 Remove from the oven and let them cool completely on the pan before storing.
