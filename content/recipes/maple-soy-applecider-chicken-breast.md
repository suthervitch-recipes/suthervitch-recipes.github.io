ingredients:
  - 3 chicken breasts
  - Salt and pepper
  - 60 g flour (1/4 cup)
  - 3 1/2 tbsp butter or 2 1/2 tbsp olive oil or any
  - 4-5 garlic cloves
  - 1 1/2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - 1 tbsp soy sauce
  - 90 g (1/3 cup) honey (or maple syrup)
  - Chives (optional)

salt and pepper chicken breasts

cross cut one side of breast

dredge in flour

fry chicken

Mince garlic

reduce heat, add butter and garlic

fry for a minute

add soy sauce, apple cider and honey

let it thicken and keep cooking chicken

plate and sprinkle chives
