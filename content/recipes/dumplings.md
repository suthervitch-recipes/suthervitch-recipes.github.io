  - ⅔ cup hot water(150 mL)

  - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  - 1.5 tbsp chicken stock or 2 frozen cube
  - '-'
  - Filling
  - 1/2 lb Ground Beef
  - 1-1/2 TBsp Soy Sauce
  - 1/2 TBsp Sugar
  - 1/2 tsp Ground White Pepper
  - 1/2 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  - 1/3 C Cilantro Chopped
  - 1-1/2 Sticks fine chopped green onion
  - 1/4 C Water or Broth
  - 1/4 tsp salt
  - 1 tsp shaoxing wine
  - 1 tsp mince ginger

Mix soup ingredients in hot water and let cool in glass container.

After cooled, cut into small cubes with a knife

Mix soup cubes with filling ingredients

Add spoonful of mix to dumpling wrapper and wrap

Steam 8-10mins or fry in oil.
